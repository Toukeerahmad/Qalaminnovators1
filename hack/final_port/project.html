Our Features

AI doctor

We developed an AI Doctor that analyzes uploaded medical images and provides professional diagnostic insights, aiding in disaster scenarios where medical access is limited. The system leverages advanced AI models to detect conditions and offer preliminary assessments. Designed for emergency response, it enhances medical accessibility and decision-making.

chat-bot

We developed an AI Chatbot that understands medical issues, explains their causes, and provides detailed prescription insights. Designed for disaster scenarios, it offers instant guidance and preliminary assessments, ensuring quick and accessible healthcare support when medical professionals are unavailable.

Disaster predictor

We developed a disaster prediction platform that analyzes historical and real-time data to forecast potential disasters. By detecting patterns and anomalies, it provides timely alerts, helping communities prepare and respond effectively to imminent threats.

Drone-system

An autonomous drone system uses GPS, IMUs, and computer vision for precise navigation and stability. Path planning algorithms like A* or RRT optimize routes while avoiding real-time obstacles. Sensor fusion integrates data from LiDAR, cameras, and other sensors for effective control and decision-making. AI and machine learning enable the drone to adapt to dynamic environments, improving efficiency and safety without operator intervention.
